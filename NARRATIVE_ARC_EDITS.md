# Narrative Arc Restructuring - Summary of Changes

## Problem Diagnosed

The original chapter ordering in Part II interrupted dramatic escalation by placing the evaluative/critical chapter (Ch7: Evaluation) in the middle of the applications, between Dreams/Memory and Cosmology. This deflated momentum at a crucial moment.

**Original Part II sequence:**
1. Ch5: Black Holes (physics-scale applications)
2. Ch6: Dreams/Memory (internal phenomena)  
3. **Ch7: Evaluation** ← *INTERRUPTION* - steps back to critique the framework
4. Ch8: Cosmology (cosmic-scale applications)
5. Ch9: Big Bang (ultimate origins)

This created a broken arc: the stakes were building (physics → mind → cosmos) but then suddenly stopped to ask "is any of this real?" before the climax.

## Solution Implemented

**Reordered Part II to:**
1. Ch5: Black Holes - horizons as boundaries of knowable reality
2. Ch6: Dreams/Memory - internal vs external dissolves  
3. **Ch7: Cosmology** (was Ch8) - universe structured for one observer
4. **Ch8: Big Bang** (was Ch9) - **CLIMAX** - observer and cosmos born together
5. **Ch9: Evaluation** (was Ch7) - **DENOUEMENT** - step back and assess

This creates proper rising action:
- **Act I (Part I, Ch 1-4)**: Build the framework with increasing boldness
- **Act II (Part II, Ch 5-8)**: Apply with escalating stakes (physics → mind → cosmos → origins)
- **Resolution (Ch 9)**: Critical reflection after the climax has passed
